Uncharted 4: A Theif's End

About The Game

Uncharted 4: A Theif' End is a phenomonal game that keeps its players invested. Nathan Drake once again sets out for a long lost treasure, becuase someone from his past comes back into his life. If you love puzzels, pirates, crazy adventures, and well written plots this is defiently a game for you. This is an emotional story that has many great twists and turns.The player can expect great puzzels, crazy cinematic set pieces, and really well done action that spices up the gameplay from time to time. This game also offers a decently fun multiplayer mode for people who like to be competitive and prefer the action of the game compared to anything else. Overall this game gives its players a great experience and was another great entry into this franchise.

Avaliable On: PS4 and PS5
