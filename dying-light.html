Dying Light

About The Game

Dying light is a first person perspective horror zombie survival game. It brought new energy to the genre with its new ideas and mechanics to work around. The story is decent and keeps the player inveested through everything that transpires in the fictional location of Haran. The player starts off as a weak barely capable survivor to someone who can parkour and fight their way with ease throughout the city. Between the parkour, weapons, zombie types, and variation in locations the player can naviagte, this game makes the apocalypse a good time. This game can also be enjoyed in Coop with a group of three friends which can be a ton of fun. Some of the best moments come from goofing around with some friends in the open world. The competitive mode is also pretty interesting putting a player in the role of a highly mutated zombie to hunt the other players. Overall this game brings a lot of fun to its players and is a must play.

Avaliable On: Xbox, PS4, and PC
