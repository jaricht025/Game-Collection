The Legend of Zelda: Breath of The Wild

About The Game

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of The Wild was a launch title for the switch. This game took the series in a different direction than the past making it an open world survival type game. A great calamity has occured in High Rule and the hero Link wakes up from a long rest with no memory of what happened. What happens next is up to the player and whether they want to follow where the game leads them or if the player choses to go off in their own direction. The game offers a varitey of main and side quests. The dungeons are also very different from previous games, but are successful with what they try to accomplish. This game is all about player choice and freedom. The player is given a vast and beautiful world to explore and its worth every minute.

Avaliable On: Wii U and Nintendo Switch
