The Last Of Us

About The Game

The Last Of Us is a gritty horror and survival game set 20 years in the future. After an outbreak of a deadly fungus causes people to mutate the world is far from being the same. Our main character Joel has been through a lot and his life becomes a lot more difficult when he sets out on a cross country jounry with a girl named Ellie. This game has a phenomonal story that keeps you invested in the characters and plot till the very end. From the intense moment to moment gameplay due to sneaking past or dealing with the infected or when other human beings are the problem the game keeps the player on their toes. The Last Of Us demonstrates just how far games have come in terms of a narrative experience. This is defiently a game you will want to play till the very end.

Avaliable On: PS4 and PS5
