Monster Hunter World

About The Game

Monster Hunter world is one of the best enteries within the Monster Hunter series. While these games are not know for having amazing cinematic stories or inspiring characters the addicting and well constructed gameplay more than makes up for it. With beautiful visuals, well designed creatures to fight, and rewarding gameplay, Monster Hunters offers many hours of play to those who choose to jump into it. PLaying with friends also makes it better. It is a very complex game with many systems for people who like in depth mechanics and it also offers a decnt challenge as the player progresses through the game. Some of the most amazing battles come after a lot of hard work and being able to stand up against insane monsters. It is more than worth the time if you enjoy RPGs.

Avaliable On: Xbox One, PS4, and PC
