Batman Arkham Knight

About The Game

Batman Arkham knight is the finale to the series that brought the super hero genre more prominence in video games. While it is not perfect it gives players a great experience and wraps up the series decently well. The gameplay has imporved amazingly throughout all the games. Whether you are sneaking around to take criminals out, fighting them head on, or using the batmobile the player gets to really experience what its like to be batman. The story holds up as well giving the player a nice playable comic story with a variety of recognizable characters for fans. This game gives players a lot to do and is one of the better super hero games.

Avaliable On: Xbox one, PS4, and PC
