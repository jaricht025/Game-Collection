Dark Souls III

About The Game

Dark Souls III is one of the most difficult games to complete and to get the best possible ending. The world building and story is hidden in item descriptions and can be picked up from the things the player finds around the world. At it's heart this game in a very strong RPG with character building being a very important aspect. every decision the player makes is important and can affect later runs. This game is known for not going easy on the player. You need to expect to die and learn from your mistakes. The gameplay is rewarding when you catch on and the feeling of beating what seemed impossible is a huge part of what draws people to this experience. Dark Souls III is not for the faint of heart or for those who do not have the patience to deal with a bit of what at the time seems like bs.

Avaliable On: Xbox one, PS4, and PC
