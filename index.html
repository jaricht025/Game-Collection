Home

Video games have been a central aspect of my life since I was young. I’ve played everything from the Sega genesis/NES to modern day consoles. They’ve influenced me in a lot of ways and have been my favorite hobby to do alone or with friends. As time has gone on I have found myself liking more games that have an amazing story or give a great single player/cooperative experience rather than playing comeptitively. Gaming is one of my biggest hobbies and will probably continue to be for years to come.

I've collected a lot of games over the years, but these happen to be some of my favorites. I continually return to these games when I am bored or do not have anything else to do. They are all great games in their own ways and are good examples of what video games should be. Whether its an RPG, open world, survival, horror, or whatever genre I think everything has its own value. Every game big or small requires skill and creativity to make, espicially if people are going to enjoy them.
