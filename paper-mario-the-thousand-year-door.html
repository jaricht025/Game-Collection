Paper Mario: The Thousand Year Door

About The Game

Paper Mario: The Thousand Year Door is an older game that still stands out today as a great game. It is an amazingly imagined RPG that sends Mario and a bunch of friends he meets on the way on a fun treasure hunt to save princess peach and with a decent twist to the story at the end it makes it a different adventure from most Mario games. The combat is fun and even though it is a turn based RPG the combat keeps the player involved with interactive prompts and the need for decent strategy. This game also has plenty of small fun side activities and exploring to do. While the world seems small and linear there are plenty of secrets for the player to uncover espicially when they gain more powers. This game is still one of the best in the Mario franchise.

Avaliable On: Gamecube
